The little pixel-font flag — content categories, "new" markers, statuses. Keep text 1–2 words, UPPERCASE.

```jsx
<Badge color="magenta">New quest</Badge>
<Badge color="cyan" variant="outline">Philosophy</Badge>
<Badge color="lime" dot>Online</Badge>
```

`color`: cyan | magenta | lime | amber | violet | red. `variant`: `solid` (neon fill) or `outline` (on surface). `dot` adds a leading status dot. For interactive, removable category chips use **Tag** instead.
